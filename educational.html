The Educational Subteam

Our educational subteam is creating pamphlets translated into Spanish target at adult populations to inform diabetic patients and their families about ways to avoid further complications. We are creating one pamphlet that covers foot ulcers, and another one that covers wound care, specifically using the Wagner Foot Ulcer scale. We are working with our community partners to figure out how to distribute these pamphlets to the community; it is likely we will be going door-to-door and disseminating copies to clinics in the community. We hope to make information on foot care readily available to reduce the incidences of amputations to begin with.



Our Progress

We switched over from Canva to Figma in the past semester in order to create a more polished and finished product. We are making our pamphlet culturally sensitive and inclusive, and are working on translating the pamphlet to Spanish
